**Platform & AI Engineer @ SK Telecom.** I turn data into value — building the **platforms** that let people work with their data effortlessly, and the **LLM-powered agents** that act on that data for them. Whether it's a self-service cluster platform or a domain-expert AI agent, the goal is the same: **shorten the distance between people and the value hidden in their data.**

### 🔭 Currently
Building a **multi-tenant, self-service data platform** that auto-provisions per-user **Spark / StarRocks** clusters on Kubernetes — operating **thousands of clusters** (hundreds concurrent). Working across the core orchestration service, the K8s operator, the API, observability, and CI/CD.
